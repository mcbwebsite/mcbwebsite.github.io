About Us

Welcome to Marrs Custom Builds, where passion meets expertise in bringing your vision to fruition. Based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, we proudly serve the greater Pittsburgh region. Our company was founded by a seasoned professional with a wealth of experience in high-quality construction, contracting, and building, our journey revolves around elevating outdoor living. With a focus on craftsmanship and quality, our founder instilled a commitment to excellence. We take pride in transforming visions into reality, creating bespoke additions that stand the test of time. Our motivation lies in the satisfaction of delivering enduring, high-quality results for you to cherish for years to come. Join us in redefining outdoor luxury, where every detail is a testament to our dedication and your lasting enjoyment.
